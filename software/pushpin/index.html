Pushpin for Pinboard

The best way to manage your bookmarks on your iPhone

Pushpin is a full-featured application for the best bookmarking site on the web, Pinboard.in.

Features

  • Full support for browsing your bookmarks, tags, and notes
  • Open bookmarks in a built-in webview, Safari, or Chrome
  • Search by tags, descriptions, or titles
  • Filter by unread, untagged, private, and public
  • Live updating whenever you add a new bookmark
  • Add new bookmarks quickly and easily

Requirements

  • Supported on iOS 5.1 +
  • iPhone 3GS, 4, 4S, 5 & iPod Touch
